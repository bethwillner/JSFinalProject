Insights

Home of the area’s best “smokey meats” and fresh cut meats. Featuring unparalleled custom butchering and wild game processing and packaging! The Meatery Meat Market, located in Crivitz Wisconsin, is a family owned meat market serving Marinette County and Northern Wisconsin with meat processing and sausage making. We have proudly served thousands of satisfied customers in Wisconsin and across the United States. You can trust The Meatery Market to provide you with fresh and great tasting meats and sausages for your next cookout or to professionally process and package your beef or pork. Stop by Today!

Our Store

We have a full retail store offering fresh and specialty cuts of beef, pork, and chicken. We also offer fresh local dairy, fresh bakery, fresh frozen seafood, homemade pizzas, deli items, many heat-and-eat products, and many varieties of homemade award-winning sausages. Feel free to call us for product information or availability or stop in and check out our store!

The Community

The company has earned over 370 State, National and State Fair awards, including 10 Best of Shows- the highest award given by the Wisconsin Association of Meat Processors in their Cured Meat Product Competition. Haen Meats has also earned a reputation that keeps customers coming back and welcomes new customers on a regular basis. Serving the entire Fox Valley area and beyond, customers come to experience the personal, friendly service of The Meatery Family, which has grown to include very loyal and dedicated employees, all of whom are committed to making your experience at Haen Meats a positive one. We invite you to come see for yourselves.
